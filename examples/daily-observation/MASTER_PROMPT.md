# Daily Observation

Create a native continuity application for a person who wants to become more
attentive to nearby life without maintaining a public nature profile.

The only primary action is to photograph one living thing and write one
sentence describing a concrete detail noticed today. Completion requires both
the image and the sentence. If the app backgrounds, loses camera permission, or
is killed first, preserve the draft locally and resume it without marking the
day complete.

The first launch must reach capture before account or recovery setup. The core
action, checkpoint, seal, local record, and return invitation must work offline.
Seal the image and note as immutable private artifacts referenced by one stable
event ID. Never derive event identity from an image digest or rewrite a sealed
photo in place.

After completion, show the photograph and sentence together once. A small local
and deterministic reflection may surface a prior observation from a similar
season. The action and accumulated field notebook must remain meaningful when
that reflection is disabled; do not require a classifier, model provider, or AI
chat.

Accumulate a private field notebook, not a social network or competitive
species count. Do not add followers, public maps, leaderboards, streak shame,
generic media CRUD, automatic cloud upload, a dashboard home, or analytics that
contain photographs, sentences, coordinates, or event records.

Full-resolution photos and notes remain application-encrypted on the device.
Exports are owner-selected. Treat EXIF and location handling as an explicit
privacy decision. Recovery of a contextual practice identity is separate from
content export and restoration.

The client owns the source, application identifiers, store accounts, signing
assets, infrastructure, domains, and data plane. Ask before creating paid
resources, altering DNS, rotating production credentials, or submitting to an
application store. The checked-in `continuity.manifest.json` is the source
contract and must change before product behavior changes.
