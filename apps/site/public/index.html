03 / rails

Deterministic underneath the magic.

AI may translate intention and enrich reflection. It does not quietly decide storage, completion, identity, payment, deployment, or ownership.

Local first

The essential action and its history remain useful without a network or model provider.

action_before_account: true

Private by default

Private practice data stays local or application-encrypted unless a boundary is explicitly named.

private_content: local

Manifest bounded

A feature that cannot be expressed as a valid manifest diff is unsupported—not quietly improvised.

unsupported ≠ bespoke

Ejectable from birth

Source, manifests, runbooks, identifiers, migrations, and credentials remain transferable.

ownership: yours
